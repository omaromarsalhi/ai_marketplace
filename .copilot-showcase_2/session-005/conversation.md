| Score Range | Decision | Description |
|-------------|----------|-------------|
| **85-100** | ‚úÖ Auto-Approve | Excellent match, image perfectly aligns with product data |
| **70-84** | ‚úÖ Auto-Approve | Good match, minor inconsistencies acceptable |
| **50-69** | ‚ùå Auto-Reject | Poor match, significant issues detected |
| **0-49** | ‚ùå Auto-Reject | Very poor match, image does not align with product |

**Common Issues Detected:**
- Image doesn't match product category
- Description mentions features not visible in image
- Price inconsistent with product quality shown
- Stock level doesn't match product presentation
- Image shows different product entirely
